IIIT Naya Raipur

Dr. SPM IIIT-Naya Raipur, established by the International Institute of Information Technology University Act, 2013 of the Government of Chhattisgarh, is a joint venture by Chhattisgarh State Government and National Thermal Power Corporation (NTPC). The institute is committed to pursue excellence in higher education, research and development in Information Technology (IT) and associated disciplines. IIIT Naya Raipur has mainly three branch in B.Tech - CSE, DSAI, ECE. IIIT Naya Raipur is contineously providing 100% placements to the students and IIIT Naya Raipur is always on the headline to the newspaper.
